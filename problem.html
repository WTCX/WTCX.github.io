PROBLEM

Millions of amputation patients need more flexible artificial limbs

How might we help people with disability, especially people who have lost their arms, to make them thrive again like healthy human being? They should be able to enjoy their daily life, play sport, use computer and other smart devices like people around them. It is not their fault to being short of arm because of predisposition, very rare diseases, severe infections and accidents. This problem must be addressed. In 2008, there were 10 million people have their arms being surgically removed worldwide. [1] After the surgery, every aspects of their lives will be affects. [2] In particular, study shows that people on average spend 40mins on imaging using their amputated limb. [4] This indicates how much they miss them and how many troubles they are experiencing.
